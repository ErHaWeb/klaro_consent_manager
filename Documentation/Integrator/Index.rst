===============
For Integrators
===============

As an integrator, you have the option of extending available
:ref:`purposes <howto-custom-purpose>` and/or :ref:`services
<howto-custom-service>`. You also have a wide range of options for influencing
the texts used in the Consent Management Modal through :ref:`custom XLIFF files
<howto-reference-xliff>` and :ref:`Fluid-enriched labels
<howto-fluid-enriched-labels>`. If you have any questions about the
integration, these may be answered in the FAQ section.
