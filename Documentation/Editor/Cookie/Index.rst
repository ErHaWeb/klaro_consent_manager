======
Cookie
======

For a **cookie**, various specifications can be made regarding designation, area of validity and the period of use. Although cookies, like the :ref:`configuration <for-editors-configuration>` and :ref:`services <for-editors-service>`, are independent records in the database, they are recorded directly in the editing screen of services that are dependent on the service.

The points in the following navigation correspond to the tabs that are available in the backend form of a Klaro service of the respective records and the subpages correspond to the palettes that are used to group related fields within a tab.
